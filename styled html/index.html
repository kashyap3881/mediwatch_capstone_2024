DiabetesGuard AI

DiabetesGuard AI is an advanced machine learning model designed to predict hospital readmissions for patients with diabetes.

By analyzing a comprehensive set of patient data, including medical history, current health metrics, treatment adherence, and socioeconomic factors, DiabetesGuard AI generates personalized risk assessments for diabetic patients. This powerful tool enables healthcare providers to identify high-risk individuals who may require additional support or interventions to prevent unnecessary hospital readmissions.

By leveraging predictive analytics, DiabetesGuard AI helps optimize patient care, reduce healthcare costs, and improve overall outcomes for individuals managing diabetes. The model's continuous learning capabilities ensure it stays up-to-date with the latest clinical insights, making it an invaluable asset in the proactive management of diabetic patient care.

Using DiabetesGuard AI is straightforward and intuitive.

First, prepare your patient data in CSV format with the required fields including demographics, medical history, current medications, and recent lab results. Click the "Upload Data" button to select and upload your CSV file.

Once uploaded, click "Make Prediction" to generate risk assessments for hospital readmission for each patient in your dataset. The model will analyze the data and provide detailed predictions that can help inform care decisions.

If you have verified outcome data, you can also contribute to improving the model by clicking "Train Model", which will use your uploaded dataset to further refine the model's predictive capabilities while maintaining patient privacy and data security.
